# 跨级别对照 03 · 偏弱作文（Soldier / General）

## 作文原文（复用 `cet4-08-band-01.md`）

> A successful great man said: "a soldier who don't want to be a general is not a good soldier". We can find that most of men want to become great man, and do great things! But an army has only one general, most of soldiers can only be a normal soldier. So should the soldiers all go back home, just because they can't become a general? Of course not! Everyone has his value in his position. An army can't win battle if has only a general but no soldiers. If you are a soldier, just do your best, stay in your position and everyone will respect you include the general. As my father, although he's working in the government as a normal public servant not an officer, but he did his best in work. He solved lots of questions for his department, and served for hundreds of people a week to help them, he feels that he's successful because his leader and all the people he served said to him: "you are a great man".
>
> So we just do our best in our work, do more for the others! We are all the great man!

**字数**：约 195 词（只分 2 段 + 首段过长，这本身是 discourse critical）

---

## 一、作为 CET-4 提交 → **8 分**（已有判定）

- 基本切题（立场模糊）
- 4+ 处 critical（`most of men` / `if has only a general` 缺主语 / `As my father` / `respect you include the general`）
- 段落结构缺陷（只有 2 段 + 首段严重超长）
- 判定：**8 分**

### 2.3 为什么是 5 档而不是 8 档

**CET-6 对 8 档的隐性门槛更严**：
- CET-4 8 档容忍"基本切题 + 相当多错误 + 有严重错误但思路可辨"
- CET-6 8 档除此之外，还要求"**段落结构不能崩塌** + **句子完整度达标**"

本文在这两条 CET-6 特有的隐性门槛上失守：
- ❌ 段落仅 2 段且首段严重超长
- ❌ 多处句子结构错乱（`if has only a general` / `respect you include the general`）

但又未至 2 档（不是"条理不清、思路紊乱、支离破碎"）：
- ✅ 能看出三段式思路（提出问题 → 论证 → 举例）
- ✅ 多数句子主谓齐全（虽然错误百出）
- ✅ 基本切题

→ **CET-6 下判 5 档**：基本切题、思想表达不清楚、连贯性差、有较多严重错误。

## 四、CET-6 视角下的升档路径（5 → 8）

1. **立场前置**：¶1 末给出清晰立场（支持/反对/有条件支持），不游移
2. **消灭核心 critical 错误**：
   - `a soldier who **doesn't** want` / `most **men**`（非 `most of men`）
   - `If it **has** only a general`（补主语）
   - `everyone will respect you, **including** the general`（修结构）
   - `**For example**, my father...`（非 `As my father`）
3. **正确分段**：至少 3-4 段（引入 / 论证 / 例证 / 结论）
4. **统一时态**：讨论观点用一般现在时，父亲例证用一般过去时
5. **减少中式直译**：`most of men` / `great man`（重复 4 次）/ `we just do our best` 整体替换
