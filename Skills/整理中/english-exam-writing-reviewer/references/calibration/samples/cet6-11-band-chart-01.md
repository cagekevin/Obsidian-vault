# 样例作文原文

The table above provides a clear comparison of students' monthly spending on books and entertainment between 2018 and 2023. Over the five-year period, the average expenditure on books has witnessed a noticeable decline from 80 yuan to 50 yuan, while spending on entertainment has risen sharply from 100 yuan to 180 yuan.

Several factors may account for this shift. First, the rise of digital platforms has made reading cheaper and more accessible. Students can easily download e-books or subscribe to online reading services at a very low cost. Second, after the pandemic, young people have shown a stronger desire to spend on leisure activities, such as concerts and movies, to release the pressure from study and work.

Personally, I believe that while it is understandable for students to invest more in entertainment, the decline in book spending is worrying. Reading plays a vital role in developing one's critical thinking and character. Thus, we should try to strike a balance between intellectual growth and leisure enjoyment.

（约 185 词）

# 人工阅卷批注（图表题 CET-6 11 档）

## 图表题三要素覆盖

| 要素 | 本文表现 | 评价 |
|------|---------|------|
| ① 描述变化 | `has witnessed a noticeable decline / risen sharply` + 具体数字 | ✅ 齐备，用词地道度明显优于 CET-4 11 档（`witness a decline`）|
| ② 分析原因 | 2 个原因（digital platforms / post-pandemic）| ✅ 齐备 |
| ③ 给出评论 | `strike a balance` 收束 | ✅ 齐备 |

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 11 档）

- **切题度**：切题
- **表达清晰度**：清楚。段落结构清晰
- **连贯性**：连贯。`Several factors may account for` / `Personally, I believe that` 等衔接较隐性，明显好于 CET-4 11 档
- **语言准确度**：2 处 tip + 1 处 warning
- **CET-6 11 档特征**：
  - ✅ 用词明显优于 CET-4 11 档：`expenditure` / `witness a decline` / `account for` / `strike a balance`
  - ⚠ 仍**缺非谓语 / 倒装**（CET-6 14 档的硬指标）
  - ⚠ 论证层次单一（两因并列，未分层）

## 标注错误

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶2 末 | `to release the pressure from study and work` | `to relieve the stress of study and work` | warning | lexical（`release pressure` 是中式搭配，英文搭配是 `relieve stress / ease pressure`）|
| ¶2 中 | `subscribe to online reading services at a very low cost` | `at minimal cost` | tip | lexical（`very low cost` 偏口语化）|
| ¶3 中 | `developing one's critical thinking and character` | `cultivating critical thinking and shaping character` | tip | lexical（`developing character` 略生硬）|

合计 0 critical + 1 warning + 2 tip，符合 CET-6 11 档"2-4 处小错"的上限。

（注：CET-6 11 档的错误容忍比 CET-4 11 档更严格）

## 为什么 11 分不是 14 分（CET-6 视角）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 档次指向 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 图表描述地道度 | `witnessed a noticeable decline` / `risen sharply` 已接近 14 档 | 14（+）|
| 句式复杂度 | **缺非谓语 + 缺倒装**（仅有 `while...`/`such as...` 并列插入）| 11（14 档硬性要求非谓语 ≥3）|
| 原因分析深度 | 两因并列，未分层（经济/技术/文化）| 11（14 档要辩证分层）|
| 评论力度 | `strike a balance` 收束地道但停留概念层 | 11（14 档要可操作建议）|
| 辩证让步 | 无（虽有 `while it is understandable` 但不是真正让步）| 11（14 档常有 `Admittedly... Yet...`）|
| 错误密度 | 1 warning + 2 tip | 11 档上限 |

→ 5 项指向 11，1 项接近 14，定 11 档（档内偏中位）。

## 为什么 11 分不是 8 分

- ✅ 用词明显超越 CET-6 8 档（对比 `cet6-08-band-01.md` 的 `internet make reading convenient`）
- ✅ 数据描述精准 + 使用高阶描述词
- ✅ 无立场摇摆
- ✅ 无 critical 错误

→ 明显优于 8 档。

## 升档路径（CET-6 图表题 11 → 14）

1. **加入非谓语结构**（CET-6 14 档硬指标，≥ 3 处）：
   - `Several factors may account for this shift` → `Viewed from multiple angles, this shift can be attributed to several intertwined factors`
   - `Students can easily download e-books` → `Students, armed with smartphones and unlimited data, can effortlessly download e-books`
   - `we should try to strike a balance` → `Only by actively cultivating reading habits alongside entertainment, can we strike a healthy balance`（兼顾倒装）
2. **原因分析分层**：
   - 技术层：数字平台崛起
   - 经济层：体验经济挤占图书预算
   - 心理层：Z 世代即时满足偏好
3. **加入辩证让步**：
   - `Admittedly, the boom in digital entertainment reflects healthy economic vitality. Yet, the parallel decline in reading spending ...`
4. **数据洞察而非复述**：
   - 添加比例计算："Book spending fell by 37.5% while entertainment surged by 80%, yielding a ratio reversal of over 3-fold"
5. **修复 3 处词语瑕疵**（release→relieve / very low→minimal / developing character→shaping character）
