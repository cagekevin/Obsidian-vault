# 样例作文原文

```
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to wholeheartedly recommend Mr. Zhang Wei for the marketing position at your esteemed company. Having known Zhang Wei as both a classmate and a close friend for over four years, I am confident that he possesses the qualities and skills required for this role.

During our time at Peking University, Zhang Wei consistently stood out for his creativity and leadership. As the head of our student marketing club, he successfully organized several large-scale campus campaigns, one of which attracted over 5,000 participants. His ability to communicate persuasively and work collaboratively has always impressed me.

In addition, Zhang Wei is diligent, responsible, and passionate about the marketing industry. He always completes his tasks ahead of schedule and is eager to take on new challenges. I am confident that he will make valuable contributions to your team.

Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

（正文约 165 词）

# 人工阅卷批注（应用文 CET-6 11 档）

## 应用文格式评分

| 格式要素 | 本文表现 | 评价 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 称呼 | `Dear Hiring Manager,` | ✅ 商务惯用，不知姓名时的标准写法 |
| 开场段（写作目的）| `I am writing to wholeheartedly recommend Mr. Zhang Wei for...` | ✅ 直入主题 + 高信号词 `wholeheartedly` |
| 身份说明 | `Having known Zhang Wei as both a classmate and a close friend for over four years` | ✅ 使用非谓语独立主格开场，CET-6 级别的写法 |
| 正文（特质+事例）| ¶2 具体事例 + ¶3 品质概括 | ✅ 有层次 |
| 联系邀请 | `Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.` | ✅ 推荐信标准结尾 |
| 署名 | `Yours sincerely, / Li Ming` | ✅ 规范 |

→ 格式**完整 + 专业**，明显优于 CET-4 11 档应用文。

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 11 档）

- **切题度**：切题。紧扣"推荐 Zhang Wei 从事营销职位"
- **表达清晰度**：清楚。身份 → 能力事例 → 品质总结 → 联系邀请，四段功能分工清楚
- **连贯性**：连贯。`Having known... / During our time... / In addition...` 衔接较隐性
- **语言准确度**：1 warning + 2 tip
- **CET-6 11 档特征**：
  - ✅ 用词正式地道：`wholeheartedly recommend` / `esteemed company` / `stood out` / `work collaboratively` / `diligent, responsible, passionate`
  - ✅ 非谓语 ×1（`Having known...`）+ 定语从句 `one of which attracted...`
  - ⚠ 仍**缺倒装**（CET-6 14 档常备）
  - ⚠ 品质描述用三个形容词堆叠（`diligent, responsible, and passionate`）略 lazy

## 标注错误

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶3 | `passionate about the marketing industry` | `passionate about the marketing field` 或直接 `about marketing` | warning | lexical（marketing 作为专业领域，通常不加 `industry`；`the marketing industry` 指具体商业领域）|
| ¶1 | `esteemed company` | 略过度客套（在美式商务语境下显得过礼）| tip | register（略 overformal）|
| ¶3 | `He always completes his tasks ahead of schedule and is eager to take on new challenges` | 两个并列动词时态一致但略 cliché | tip | style |

合计 0 critical + 1 warning + 2 tip，符合 CET-6 11 档"2-4 处小错"。

## 为什么 11 分不是 14 分（CET-6 视角）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 档次指向 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 书信格式 | 规范 + 专业 | 14（格式达 14 档基准）|
| 非谓语 / 倒装 | 非谓语 ×1（`Having known...`），**无倒装** | 11（CET-6 14 档需 ≥ 3 非谓语 + 倒装）|
| 品质描述 | 三个形容词堆叠（`diligent, responsible, passionate`）| 11（14 档应将品质嵌入具体事例）|
| 事例深度 | 一个事例（5,000 人活动）但单薄 | 11（14 档应 2+ 事例且有量化）|
| 用词地道度 | `wholeheartedly` / `stood out` / `work collaboratively` 已接近 14 档 | 14（+）|
| 语域一致性 | 整体正式 | 14（14 档无失） |

→ 4 项指向 11，2 项接近 14，综合定 11 档（档内偏上位）。

## 为什么 11 分不是 8 分

- ✅ 格式完美无漏项
- ✅ 用词正式地道（`wholeheartedly` / `esteemed` / `stood out`）
- ✅ 有具体事例（`5,000 participants`）
- ✅ 无 critical 错误

→ 明显优于 8 档。

## 升档路径（CET-6 应用文 11 → 14）

1. **加入倒装**（CET-6 14 档常见于强调句）：
   - `I am confident that he possesses the qualities and skills required` → `Rarely have I encountered a candidate who so fully embodies the qualities your position demands`
2. **加入更多非谓语**（≥ 3 处）：
   - `He always completes his tasks ahead of schedule` → `Completing every assignment ahead of schedule, Zhang Wei has repeatedly set the standard for his peers`
3. **品质嵌入事例**（而非平铺罗列）：
   - 把 `diligent, responsible, and passionate` 拆进 3 个微型事例（比如：`His diligence is reflected in his 3 am drafts during the spring festival campaign; his sense of responsibility was evident when he...`）
4. **替换过度客套词**：
   - `esteemed company` → `the dynamic team at [Company Name]`
   - `wholeheartedly recommend` → `recommend with great enthusiasm`（更自然）
5. **量化事例**：
   - `over 5,000 participants` → `over 5,000 participants, generating 12M RMB in brand impressions`
