# 样例作文原文

With the development of internet, people's way of reading has been changed a lot. Some people think internet is good for reading, but others think it brings bad influence. In my opinion, both sides have their reason.

On one hand, internet make reading more convenient. We can read news, books and articles on our phone anytime and anywhere. It's also cheaper than buy paper books. What's more, there are many free resources online which we can learn from, this is very helpful for students.

On the other hand, reading on screen for long time is bad for our eyes, and many people can't focus when there is too many information. They just skim the content and don't think deeply. This is why some experts said that deep reading ability is declining among young people nowadays.

So we should use internet wisely. When we read serious things, maybe traditional book is still better. But when we want to know information quickly, internet is helpful. Only by combining two methods, we can enjoy both advantages.

（约 180 词）

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 8 档基准）

- **切题度**：**基本切题**（不是"切题"）。主题方向对，但立场模糊——`both sides have their reason` 后全文一直骑墙，直到最后一段才提出 `use internet wisely`，立场出现滞后
- **表达清晰度**：有些地方**不够清楚**（¶2 末 `which we can learn from, this is very helpful` 逗号衔接两个独立句、¶4 末倒装规则错）
- **连贯性**：**勉强连贯**。段落分明但段内句子跳跃，`On one hand / On the other hand / So` 衔接机械
- **语言准确度**：**相当多错误，有严重错误**（4 处 critical）

## 严重错误（critical，触发 8 档关键）

1. ¶1 首 `With the development of internet` → `With the development of the Internet`（冠词缺失 + Internet 大小写）critical
2. ¶2 首 `internet make reading` → `the Internet makes reading`（主谓一致 + 冠词）critical
3. ¶2 第 3 句 `cheaper than buy paper books` → `cheaper than buying paper books`（than 后动名词 vs 不定式）critical
4. ¶4 末 `Only by combining two methods, we can enjoy both advantages` → `Only by combining the two methods can we enjoy both advantages`（倒装规则错 + 冠词缺失）critical

## 其它错误（warning / tip 级）

- ¶1 末 `both sides have their reason` → `have their reasons`（单复数）warning
- ¶2 末 `there is too many information` → `there is too much information`（可数/不可数）critical（其实也可算第 5 处 critical）
- ¶2 末 `which we can learn from, this is very helpful` → 逗号拼接两句（run-on sentence）warning
- ¶3 末 `some experts said` → `have said`（时态）warning
- ¶4 `maybe traditional book is still better` → `traditional books are still better`（冠词/单复数）warning

## 为什么 8 分不是 11 分（关键边界）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 11 档要求（CET-6）|
|-------|---------|---------|
| 切题 | 基本切题（立场骑墙到 ¶4 才清晰）| 切题（立场一致且早期亮出）|
| 严重错误 | ≥ 4 处 critical | ≤ 1 处 |
| 表达清晰 | 局部不够清楚（run-on + 倒装规则错）| 清楚 |
| 倒装规则 | `Only by... , we can...` 错（正确应为 `Only by... can we...`）| 11 档一般不强求倒装，但若用必须正确 |
| 段落结构 | 4 段分明但段内跳跃 | 结构清晰 |

→ 5 项指向 8 档，明显不达 11 档。关键：**倒装规则错**在 CET-6 层面是典型的"想用复杂句但驾驭不住"的信号。

## 为什么 8 分不是 5 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 5 档描述 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 基本切题 | ✓ 是 | ✓ |
| 表达思想不清楚 | "不够清楚"（局部）| 不清楚（大部分）|
| 连贯性 | 勉强连贯 | 连贯性差 |
| 严重错误占比 | ~20-25%（4-5 处 critical / ~20 句）| ≥ 30% |
| 论证结构 | 可辨认三段式 | 结构失控 |

→ 5 项指向 8（优于 5），全文仍有可识别的对比论证结构。

## 升档路径（CET-6 8 → 11）

1. **消除所有 critical 级错误**（重点：冠词、主谓一致、倒装规则）
   - `With the development of **the** Internet`
   - `**The** Internet **makes** reading more convenient`
   - `cheaper than **buying** paper books`
   - `Only by combining **the two methods** **can we** enjoy both advantages`
2. **立场前置**：¶1 末直接表态（"支持/反对/有条件支持"），不留到 ¶4
3. **修复 run-on**：`...which we can learn from**.** **This** is very helpful for students.`
4. **补充具体例证**（¶2 或 ¶3）：举一个自己或身边人的阅读案例，不能全是抽象陈述
5. **替换 `maybe` 等口语词**为书面语（`arguably` / `in most cases`）

# 该样例用途

- **Step 3 定档**：**CET-6 8 档**典型样例
- **边界判定**：CET-6 8 vs 11（最关键的争议边界，尤其对"倒装规则错"的严惩）
- **Step 4 档内调节**：作为"4 处 critical + 基本切题 + 立场骑墙"的锚点
- **批改教学价值**：CET-6 考生最常见的陷阱——"想用高级句式但规则错"（倒装、非谓语逻辑主语错等）→ 此样例是该问题的典型案例
