## 升档路径（→ 第四档 10–12）

1. **图表动词升档**（见 writing-vocabulary.md 第四节）：
   - `is going up → rose steadily`
   - `become → climbed to`
   - `is less and less → dwindled / underwent a gradual decline`
2. **修基础语法错误**（iss-1 / iss-2 / iss-3）
3. **加阶段性描述**：`The decline of offline revenue saw an acceleration around 2020, dropping from 420 billion to 360 billion within the following five years.`
4. **衔接升级**：`But → whereas / In contrast`；`So → Consequently`
5. **词汇升档**（≤5 处）：
   - `cheap → affordable` / `expensive → pricier`
   - `things → products / merchandise`
   - `change → pivot / transform`
   - `work together → embrace synergy`
6. **语域 formal 化**：`In my opinion → From my perspective / It is my belief that`
