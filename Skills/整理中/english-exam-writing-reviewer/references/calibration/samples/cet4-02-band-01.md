# 样例作文原文

Nowadays, everybody want do great things, such as student we would rather to elect the hot of saucer rather than study the useful teknologe. But, as show in the eassyth at it saying "If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way".

There are some reasons we should do some small things. To begin with, nothing is distinguish by great things and small things. To sum up the small thing. It is also a great thing. And the great thing is own to people's opinion. Then, if want to be success, the small things will help to you. Furthermore, the most of all famous people is grown in small things. Finally, the things must said to your self. If you unable to do, you should give up quickly.

In short, it is time to do some things no matter how great things or small things. When we growing up, we are get benefited from those small things. Every is important. Every things is benefit to your future. Only by this way we can successful and more happy.

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断

- **条理不清、思路紊乱**：¶2 连续 6 个要点互不关联，且每条观点自相矛盾
- **语言支离破碎**：约 15 个句子中，≥ 10 句存在严重结构错误
- **大部分句子都有严重错误**：主谓一致、时态、冠词、用词、拼写全面失控
- **对话意图不明**：最后一段 "Every is important. Every things is benefit..." 句子破碎

## 严重错误（critical，占比 ≥ 70%）

1. `everybody want do great things`（动词缺失 + 主谓）
2. `we would rather to elect the hot of saucer`（`hot of saucer` 用词完全错误 + 不定式错）
3. `as show in the eassyth at it saying`（拼写 + 句法完全错乱）
4. `nothing is distinguish by`（动词形式错）
5. `To sum up the small thing. It is also a great thing`（句号分裂 + 逻辑断）
6. `the great thing is own to people's opinion`（用词错）
7. `if want to be success, the small things will help to you`（从句缺主语 + 词性错）
8. `the most of all famous people is grown in small things`（冠词 + 主谓 + 逻辑）
9. `the things must said to your self`（被动语态错）
10. `If you unable to do, you should give up quickly`（缺系动词 + 逻辑反题）
11. `When we growing up, we are get benefited`（时态全面错乱）
12. `Every is important. Every things is benefit`（冠词/单复数/词性全错）
13. `Only by this way we can successful and more happy`（倒装/动词缺失）

## 拼写错误

- `saucer`（应为 science？） / `teknologe`（technology）/ `eassyth`（essay？）/ `hot of saucer`（热门专业？）

## 为什么 2 分不是 5 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 5 档描述 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 基本切题 | ❌ 立场摇摆，建议"should give up quickly" 直接反题意 | 基本切题 |
| 论证结构 | 6 个要点互相矛盾、无递进 | 可辨认结构 |
| 句子完整度 | ≥ 70% 句子严重残缺 | 多数句子完整 |

→ 4 项全部指向 2 档。

## 为什么 2 分不是 0 分

- 虽然条理不清，但**能看出是在谈"great things / small things"主题**
- 不是白卷、不是仅孤立词、不是完全离题
- 因此判 2 档（1-3 分），而非 0 分

## 升档路径（2 → 5）

1. **先保证每个句子完整**：主语 + 谓语 + 宾语齐全，不写残缺从句
2. **立场一致**：选一边（支持/反对），全文围绕同一立场展开
3. **至少 3 个可识别段落**：引入 / 论证 / 结论
4. **消灭最明显的 5 处严重错误**（特别是主谓一致、时态）
5. **不要堆砌机械衔接词**（to begin with / furthermore / finally）而无实质内容
