# 样例作文原文

As is vividly shown in the picture, a young man is walking with his eyes glued to the small screen of his smartphone, completely unaware of the spectacular sunset unfolding right before him. The picture, though simple, conveys a striking message about how technology is reshaping our relationship with the world.

Undoubtedly, smartphones have brought enormous convenience to our lives. However, when we are absorbed in endless scrolling, we risk losing the capacity to appreciate the beauty of real life. The sunset in the picture symbolizes countless precious moments — a friend's smile, the first snow of winter, or a family dinner — that are quietly passing us by.

It is high time that we looked up from our screens. Only by striking a balance between the digital world and real life can we truly live rather than merely exist.

（约 145 词）

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-4 14 档）

- **切题度**：切题。画面描述精准 + 立意深刻
- **表达清晰度**：思想清楚。三段式"画面 → 解读 → 评论"分工明确
- **连贯性**：通顺 + 连贯。`though simple` / `However` / `It is high time` 构成隐性衔接
- **语言准确度**：2 处 tip 级小瑕
- **CET-4 14 档特征全部达标**：
  - ✅ **非谓语 ×2**：`glued to` / `unfolding` / `absorbed in`
  - ✅ **倒装**：`Only by striking a balance... can we truly live`
  - ✅ **虚拟语气**：`It is high time that we looked up`
  - ✅ **高级搭配**：`glued to` / `spectacular` / `absorbed in` / `endless scrolling` / `striking a balance`
  - ✅ **破折号插入具体意象**（`a friend's smile, the first snow of winter, or a family dinner`）

## 标注的小瑕（对应 14 档"仅有个别小错"）

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶1 末 | `reshaping our relationship with the world` | `reshaping our relationship with the real world` | tip | lexical（`the world` 略空泛，加 `real` 更扣题）|

## 为什么 14 分不是 15 分（档内 ±1）

`delta = 0`（给档中位 14）的理由：

- 三要素齐全 + 画面描述精准 + 立意直抵技术哲学层
- 句式高级（非谓语 + 倒装 + 虚拟语气三件套齐活）
- 具体意象（`a friend's smile, the first snow of winter, or a family dinner`）让评论不落空
- 但在 "`relationship with the world`" 处略空泛，未达到"零瑕顶格"15 分水平
