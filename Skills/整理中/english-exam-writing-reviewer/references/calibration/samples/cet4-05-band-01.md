# 样例作文原文

As show in this saying, Recentally many people, especially young person, think it is certainly do good to do great things, rather than small things. Moreover, they often think do small things has no value and boring.

The saying above tries to criticize the phenomenon that most people want do great things rather than small things. It's really a wrong views. After carefully consideration, I have state that the saying is more biased. It given following reasons. Firstly, There not only great things but also small things in every project. Besides, the small things can do very excellent too, if you can make your minds to do it. Further, If you can’t do a small things well, neither great things.

From what discussed above, we conclud that question quite depend on choice. In my own eyes, no matter what ability we have, how excellent we are. Because if you want walk thousands miles, you have to do every step in you foot. Only if do small things well, do great things in the same time.

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断

- **切题度**：**基本切题**。主题方向对但 ¶2 后半"saying is more biased"与原题立场抵牾，逻辑反复
- **表达清晰度**：**不清楚**（已不再是"不够清楚"）。多个句子读两遍仍不明意
- **连贯性**：**差**。`Firstly / Besides / Further` 机械衔接但无实质逻辑递进
- **语言准确度**：**较多严重错误**

## 严重错误（critical，占比 ≥ 30%）

1. `As show in this saying`（非谓语错误）
2. `think it is certainly do good to do great things`（句子结构错乱）
3. `think do small things has no value and boring`（动词缺失 + 搭配）
4. `It's really a wrong views`（单复数矛盾）
5. `I have state that`（时态 + 词性错）
6. `It given following reasons`（句子残缺 + 时态）
7. `the saying is more biased`（用词不当，与作者立场矛盾）
8. `the small things can do very excellent too`（结构错乱）
9. `no matter what ability we have, how excellent we are. Because...`（句号后紧接从句残缺）
10. `do small things well, do great things in the same time`（逻辑错位）

## 其它错误

- `Recentally` / `conclud` / `personlly`（拼写）
- `young person` → `young people`
- `i think we should`（大小写）

## 为什么 5 分不是 2 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 2 档描述 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 基本切题 | ✓（勉强）| 条理不清 |

## 升档路径（5 → 8）

1. 先保"基本切题 + 立场一致"：开篇直接表态（支持/反对），不左右摇摆
2. 消灭 3-5 处最严重的结构性错误
3. 统一时态（全文用一般现在时讨论观点）
4. 替换中式直译（`do great things` 循环使用 → 换同义表达）
5. 字数达到 120 词下限（当前约 190 词，字数够，关键在清晰度）
