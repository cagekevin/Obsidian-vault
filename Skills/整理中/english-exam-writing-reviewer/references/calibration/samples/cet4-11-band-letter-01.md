# 样例作文原文

```
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing this letter to offer some suggestions on improving the services of our university library. As a frequent visitor, I really love the library, but I also notice a few things that could be better.

First of all, the opening hours are not long enough. Many students want to study after 10 pm, especially before exams, but the library closes too early. I suggest that the library extend the closing time to 11 pm during exam periods. Secondly, the number of seats is limited. Students often have to wait for a long time to get a seat. It would be great if more study rooms could be opened. Finally, some popular books have only one or two copies, which makes it hard to borrow them.

I believe these small changes will make the library much more helpful for us. Thank you for considering my suggestions.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

（正文约 155 词，不含称呼与署名）

# 人工阅卷批注（应用文 CET-4 11 档）

## 应用文格式评分（独立评分维度）

| 格式要素 | 本文表现 | 评价 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 称呼（Salutation） | `Dear Sir or Madam,` | ✅ 规范（不知收件人具体姓名时的标准写法）|
| 开场段（Purpose） | `I am writing this letter to offer some suggestions on...` | ✅ 直入主题，符合书信套语 |
| 正文（Body） | 3 段式（总 + 3 点建议 + 收束）| ✅ 条理清晰 |
| 结尾客套 | `Thank you for considering my suggestions.` | ✅ 符合商务书信收尾规范 |
| 署名（Signature） | `Yours sincerely, / Li Ming` | ✅ 与 `Dear Sir or Madam` 匹配（不知姓名→sincerely；知名→faithfully，规则反过来有争议但 sincerely 更常用）|

→ 格式**规范无漏项**，这是应用文 11 档的基础门槛。

## 隐含维度诊断

- **切题度**：切题。紧扣"图书馆服务改进建议"
- **表达清晰度**：思想清楚。3 条建议并列清晰
- **连贯性**：连贯。`First of all / Secondly / Finally` 机械衔接
- **语言准确度**：2 处 tip + 1 处 warning
- **应用文特有观察**：
  - ⚠ 语域（register）偏口语——`I really love the library` / `It would be great` 不够正式
  - ⚠ 缺具体数据支持（如"每天排队 20 分钟"）

## 标注错误

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶1 末 | `I really love the library` | `I have great appreciation for its resources` | warning | register（口语化，不适合给管理方的正式书信）|
| ¶2 中 | `It would be great if more study rooms could be opened` | `It would be greatly appreciated if more study rooms could be made available` | tip | register（口语化 + 被动语态偏生硬）|
| ¶3 首 | `I believe these small changes will make the library much more helpful` | `I trust these modest adjustments will significantly enhance the library's usefulness` | tip | lexical（`small changes` 口语，`helpful` 偏幼稚）|

合计 0 critical + 1 warning（语域）+ 2 tip，符合 CET-4 11 档"少量语言错误"。

## 为什么 11 分不是 14 分（应用文视角）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 档次指向 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 书信格式 | 规范齐全 | 14 |
| 语域一致性 | 局部口语化（`really love` / `would be great`）| 11（14 档应通篇正式）|
| 建议建设性 | 3 条建议但泛泛，缺具体数据 | 11（14 档应有数据 + 可操作方案）|
| 句式复杂度 | `which makes it hard to borrow them` 定语从句 ×1，无非谓语/倒装 | 11 |
| 衔接方式 | 机械 `First of all / Secondly / Finally` | 11（14 档用 `To begin with / Another concern worth raising / Last but equally important`）|

→ 5 项指向 11，定 11 档。

## 为什么 11 分不是 8 分

- ✅ 格式零漏项（8 档常漏称呼/署名/结尾客套）
- ✅ 主题清晰 + 3 条建议具体可辨
- ✅ 无 critical 语法错误
- ✅ 语气虽偶口语但整体礼貌得体

→ 明显优于 8 档。

## 升档路径（CET-4 应用文 11 → 14）

1. **升级语域**：
   - `I really love the library` → `As a regular patron, I hold the library in high regard`
   - `the library closes too early` → `the current closing hour falls short of students' study needs`
   - `It would be great if` → `I would greatly appreciate it if`
   - `small changes` → `modest adjustments`
2. **数据支撑**（应用文 14 档硬指标）：
   - `wait for a long time` → `wait an average of 20 minutes during peak hours`
   - `only one or two copies` → `a single copy of popular titles such as... has been shared among 50+ reservations`
3. **衔接升级**：
   - `First of all → Secondly → Finally` → `To begin with → Another issue worth raising → Last but equally important`
4. **加入非谓语结构**：
   - `Many students want to study after 10 pm, especially before exams` → `Many students, especially those preparing for exams, wish to continue studying past 10 pm`
5. **收尾强化**：
   - 加一句表达愿意参与改进：`I would be more than willing to participate in any follow-up discussions should you find these suggestions worth considering.`
