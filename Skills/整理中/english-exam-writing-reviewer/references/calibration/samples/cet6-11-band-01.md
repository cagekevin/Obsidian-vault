# 样例作文原文

Lifelong learning is a concept that has drawn increasing attention in recent years. Many people believe that we should never stop learning even after we finish school, and I strongly agree with this view.

There are several reasons why lifelong learning matters. First of all, the society is changing very quickly. New technologies appear every year, and the knowledge we learned in college may soon be outdated. If we stop learning after graduation, we will easily be left behind by others. For example, my uncle used to work in a traditional factory. When automation came, he had to learn computer skills at the age of 45. Thanks to his study, he successfully kept his job.

What's more, learning makes our life more colorful. Reading books, learning a new language or picking up a new hobby can bring us great joy, which also keeps our brain active.

In conclusion, lifelong learning is not only a way to keep up with the times, but also a source of happiness. We should take it as a habit for our whole life.

（约 172 词）

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 11 档基准）

- **切题度**：切题。主题清晰（lifelong learning 重要性）+ 立场一致（`I strongly agree`）
- **表达清晰度**：思想清楚。主题句 → 两大理由（社会/自我）→ 例证（uncle）→ 结论
- **连贯性**：文字连贯，但衔接略机械（`First of all` / `What's more` / `In conclusion` 显性连词堆砌）
- **语言准确度**：有少量错误（3 处），均不影响理解
- **CET-6 11 档特征**：
  - ✅ 论证结构完整
  - ⚠ 无非谓语 / 倒装（仅定语从句 `which also keeps our brain active`）→ CET-6 11 档的典型短板
  - ⚠ 词汇偏常用（`very quickly` / `great joy` / `colorful`）→ 距 CET-6 14 档的"地道 + 丰富"有差距

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |

## 升档路径（CET-6 11 → 14）

1. **用词升级**：
   - `very quickly` → `at an unprecedented pace`
   - `great joy` → `profound fulfillment` / `intellectual delight`
   - `colorful` → `enriched` / `multi-dimensional`
   - `keep up with the times` → `stay relevant in a volatile era`
2. **加入非谓语结构**（CET-6 14 档硬性要求 ≥ 3 处）：
   - `Many people believe that...` → `Widely acknowledged as a prerequisite for career longevity, lifelong learning...`
   - `When automation came, he had to...` → `Confronted with automation, he had to...`
3. **加入倒装**（至少 1 处）：
   - `We should take it as a habit...` → `Only by taking it as a lifelong habit can we truly thrive in this era`
4. **加入辩证让步**：
   - 在 ¶4 前加 `Admittedly, lifelong learning demands considerable effort. Yet...`
5. **替换机械衔接**：
   - `First of all` → 直接用主题句切入
   - `What's more` → `Beyond professional necessity, ...`
   - `In conclusion` → `In essence, ...`
6. **修复 3 处小错**（冠词 + 冗余 + 搭配）
