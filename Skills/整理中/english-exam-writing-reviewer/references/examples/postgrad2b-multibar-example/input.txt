[prompt]
Write an essay of about 150 words based on the bar chart below, in which you should:
1) describe the chart, and
2) give your comments.
The chart compares Chinese urban residents' monthly spending on three categories in
2019 and 2024 (¥ per household): Food 1800→2200; Education 600→1400; Entertainment
500→1100.

[essay]
The bar chart compares the monthly expenditure of Chinese urban households on three
major items, namely food, education and entertainment, in 2019 and 2024. Three
noteworthy similarities and differences can be identified.

On one hand, all three categories recorded an upward trajectory, suggesting a
broad-based rise in household purchasing power. On the other hand, the magnitude
varies dramatically: food spending rose modestly by roughly 22%, from ¥1800 to
¥2200; education spending more than doubled, climbing from ¥600 to ¥1400; while
entertainment spending also more than doubled, increasing from ¥500 to ¥1100.

Two underlying factors are worth considering. Firstly, the growing emphasis on
children's academic training has pushed education costs up sharply. Secondly, as
material needs are increasingly satisfied, urban residents are channelling a
larger share of disposable income into leisure and cultural consumption.

Taken together, the pattern signals a transition from subsistence-oriented to
quality-oriented consumption.
