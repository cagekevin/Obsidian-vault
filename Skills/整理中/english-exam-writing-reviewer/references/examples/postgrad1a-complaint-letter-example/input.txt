[prompt]
Directions: Write a letter of about 100 words to the customer service manager of an
online bookstore to complain about a book you have recently received, explain the
problem and propose a solution. Use "Li Ming" instead of your real name. (10 points)

[essay]
Dear Manager,

I am writing to lodge a formal complaint regarding the copy of "Principles of
Macroeconomics" (Order No. 20240815-AX237) that I received from your online
bookstore on 15 August 2024. The cover was badly dented and approximately 30
pages showed clear water damage, rendering the textbook barely usable for my
upcoming semester.

Given the book was purchased at 198 RMB and listed as "new", I hope you may
either arrange a full refund or dispatch a prompt replacement at your own cost.
Considering that term begins on 2 September, an early response would be much
appreciated.

I look forward to a prompt resolution of this matter.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
