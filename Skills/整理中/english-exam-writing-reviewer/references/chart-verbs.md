## 一、7 种子类型速查表

| chart_subtype | 中文 | 典型考题 | 核心描述动作 | 高频失误点 |
|--------------|-----|---------|-------------|-----------|
| `bar_chart` | 柱状图（单）| 2018、2020、2023 英二 | **对比离散类别** | 只读数值不分类别；忽略最大/最小对比 |
| `pie_chart` | 饼图（单）| 2017、2021 英二 | **占比 + 比例关系** | 漏掉"合计 100%"隐含约束 |
| `table` | 表格 | 2024 英二（首次）| **逐行/逐列 + 交叉对比** | 只做行汇总忽略列对比 |
| `line_graph` | 折线图 | 2025 英二 | **时间趋势** | 只说"上升/下降"不分段 |
| `multi_bar` | 双/多柱对比 | 2019 英二 | **类别 × 变量双重对比** | 只描述一组柱，忽略对比维度 |
| `multi_pie` | 双/多饼对比 | 2022 英二 | **跨时期占比结构变化** | 只说"变大变小"不说"结构迁移" |
| `mixed` | 混合（图+表 / 表+柱）| 预留 | **先分工描述再综合** | 结构混乱，主次不分 |

## 二、按子类型的专属描述动词

### 2.1 `bar_chart`（单柱状）—— 对比 + 比较级

| 功能 | Mid-tier | High-tier | Academic-tier |
|------|---------|-----------|---------------|
| 最高柱 | is the highest | tops the chart at | constitutes the largest share |
| 最低柱 | is the lowest | sits at the bottom | represents the smallest segment |
| 对比 | A is higher than B | A exceeds B by X% | A outstrips / outpaces B |
| 差距 | the difference is X | a gap / disparity of X | a margin / differential of X |

**典型句式**：

> As illustrated in the bar chart, category A, accounting for N%, towers above the rest, while category B, at merely M%, lags far behind, leaving a gap of (N-M) percentage points between them.

### 2.2 `pie_chart`（单饼图）—— 占比

| 功能 | Mid-tier | High-tier |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 最大块 | takes up the largest part | accounts for the lion's share of |
| 占比 | X% of | constitutes / comprises X% of |
| 比例关系 | is twice as much as | is twofold / threefold that of |
| 剩余 | the rest | the remainder / the remaining fraction |

**典型句式**：

> The pie chart reveals that A accounts for the lion's share at N%, followed by B at M%, with C making up the remaining (100-N-M)%.

### 2.3 `table`（表格）—— 交叉对比

**核心技巧**：**不能逐格读数**，必须归纳行/列趋势。

| 功能 | Mid-tier | High-tier |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 归纳行 | across all rows | throughout all categories |
| 归纳列 | for each column | for every indicator |
| 最值 | the highest / lowest value | the peak / trough value |
| 平均 | on average | the average figure stands at |
| 例外 | except for | with the notable exception of |

**典型句式**：

> The table contrasts X across N categories (rows) and M indicators (columns). It is revealing that, on average, category A consistently tops the chart, with the notable exception of indicator M3, where category B surges ahead.

### 2.4 `line_graph`（折线图）—— 时间趋势 + 分段

| 功能 | Mid-tier | High-tier |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 总趋势上升 | rose overall | exhibited an upward trajectory |
| 总趋势下降 | fell overall | charted a downward trend |
| 波动 | went up and down | fluctuated / oscillated |
| 快速增长 | rose rapidly | surged / climbed steeply |
| 缓慢增长 | rose slowly | edged up / inched upward |
| 达到峰值 | reached the highest point | peaked at / hit its zenith at |
| 触底 | reached the lowest point | bottomed out at / sank to a trough |
| 分段 | then / after that | in the subsequent phase / over the ensuing years |

**典型句式**（**必须分段**，不能只说"总体上升"）：

> Between Year 1 and Year 3, figure X climbed gradually from A to B; subsequently, it surged to its zenith of C in Year 5, before edging back to D by Year 7.

### 2.5 `multi_bar`（双/多柱对比）—— 类别 × 变量

**核心技巧**：必须先说**两组的相似点**，再说**差异点**。不能只读一组。

| 功能 | High-tier |
|------|-----------|
| 相似 | parallel each other / display a similar pattern |
| 差异 | diverge significantly / follow contrasting trajectories |
| 两组共同最高项 | both peak at X |
| 两组共同最低项 | share X as their lowest segment |
| 交叉点 | intersect at / converge on |

**典型句式**：

> The dual bar chart juxtaposes A-group and B-group across N categories. **While both groups peak at category X, they diverge markedly at category Y**, where A-group (N%) towers above B-group (M%), reflecting a structural shift.

### 2.6 `multi_pie`（多饼对比）—— 结构迁移

**核心技巧**：重点在"**结构变化方向**"（哪一块变大/变小），不是单一数据。

| 功能 | High-tier |
|------|-----------|
| 结构迁移 | a structural shift toward / away from |
| 比重扩大 | has expanded its share by X points |
| 比重萎缩 | has contracted from N% to M% |
| 反映趋势 | reflects a broader trend of |

**典型句式**：

> Comparing the two pie charts across the decade, the most striking change is the **structural shift toward digital media**, whose share has expanded from N% to M%, while traditional media has correspondingly contracted by (N-M) percentage points.

### 2.7 `mixed`（混合）—— 主次分工

**核心技巧**：先决定**主次**（哪个图是主要论据），再用"in addition / furthermore"补充次要图。

## 三、跨子类型通用元素

### 开头总述（适用所有 chart_subtype）

| 层级 | 模板 |
|------|------|
| 常规 | As can be seen from the chart, ... |
| 进阶 | The chart vividly illustrates / depicts... |
| 高阶 | A cursory glance at the above chart reveals that... |
| 学术 | The statistics presented herein offer a panoramic view of... |

### 结尾归因 + 对策

| 层级 | 模板 |
|------|------|
| 进阶 | The fluctuation can be attributed to several factors. |
| 高阶 | The underlying reasons behind this trend are multifaceted / threefold. |
| 对策 | To redress this imbalance, concerted efforts from multiple stakeholders are imperative. |

## 四、数据精准度检查清单（Skill 必做）

针对考研英二 B 节，`dimension_diagnosis.task_completion` 里必须逐条核对：

1. **所有关键数据点是否被提及**（不能漏最高/最低值）
2. **百分比/单位是否与图一致**（`percent` / `%` / `million` / `billion` 不能混用）
3. **年份/时期是否准确**（eg. "in 2020" vs "by 2020"，in 指当年，by 指截止）
4. **上下文关系**（对比 / 因果 / 时间先后）是否正确

若出现**数据错位** ≥ 2 处 → 在 `issues` 中标记 `type: "data_inaccuracy"` `severity: "critical"`，并在 `dimension_diagnosis.task_completion` 里降档。

## 五、每种子类型的常见扣档失误速查

| chart_subtype | Critical 失误（直接降档）| Warning 失误（档内扣分）|
|--------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| `bar_chart` | 漏掉 1/3 以上类别 | 只读一个数据不做对比 |
| `pie_chart` | 百分比总和错（如列出来 > 120%）| 漏掉"其它" wedge |
| `table` | 只读一行/一列就结束 | 没有平均/最值归纳 |
| `line_graph` | 漏掉峰值或谷值 | 不分段，笼统"上升/下降" |
| `multi_bar` | 完全忽略另一组 | 对比没找到差异点 |
| `multi_pie` | 只说"变大"不量化 | 未提"结构迁移" |
| `mixed` | 主次颠倒（把次要图当主线）| 两图结论脱节 |
