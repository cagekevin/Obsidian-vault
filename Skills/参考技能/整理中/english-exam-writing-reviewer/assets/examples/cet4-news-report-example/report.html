题目

Write a news report to your campus newspaper on a campus blood donation event that took place last weekend. You should include the time, place, organizer, participants and its significance. 120–180 words.

作文

Last Saturday morning, a campus-wide blood donation event was successfully held on the square in front of the library at Nankai University. Co-organized by the Red Cross Society and the Student Union, the activity attracted more than 400 undergraduates, postgraduates and faculty members within four hours. According to Zhang Wei, the chief coordinator, the donated blood will be transferred to Tianjin Municipal Blood Center and used for emergency medical rescue in the coming weeks. Many participants reported that the pre-registration and on-site health check were efficient, and free juice and biscuits were provided afterwards. The event demonstrates growing social responsibility among young students and also alleviates the seasonal blood shortage that local hospitals typically face in summer. Further donation sessions are scheduled for next month, which, according to the organizers, will be extended to neighbouring campuses.
