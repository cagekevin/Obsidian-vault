附录：题目 & 作文原文

题目

Write an essay of 160-200 words based on the picture below. In your essay, you should: 1) describe the picture briefly, 2) interpret its intended meaning, and 3) give your comments. [Picture: A young man stands at a crossroads at dusk. One signpost points to a brightly lit city skyline labelled "Instant Success"; the other points to a narrow uphill path winding toward distant mountains, labelled "Long Journey of Mastery". Below the picture: "捷径少人走，远路终见山" (Shortcuts are crowded; the long road reaches the peak).]

作文

In the picture, a young man is standing at a crossroad when night is coming. On his left, there is a sign pointing to a bright city which says "Instant Success". On his right, there is another sign pointing to a narrow path going up to the mountain, with the words "Long Journey of Mastery". Under the picture, there are some Chinese words which mean "Shortcuts are crowded while the long road reaches the peak". This picture tell us a truth about how to grow up. Many young people today want to become successful very quickly. They see short videos of rich CEOs and think they can do the same. However, real success often need many years of hard practice. The bright city may look attractive, but it is also full of people who give up easily. In my opinion, we should choose the long road. Only by working hard step by step can we really master a skill and become a person with real ability. Instant success is usually fragile, but long journey make us strong. So young people should be patient.
