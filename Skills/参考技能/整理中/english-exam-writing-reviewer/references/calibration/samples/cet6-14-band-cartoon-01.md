# 样例作文原文

The cartoon depicts a poignant scene: a young man, his gaze fixed on the glowing screen of his smartphone, strides past a magnificent sunset that bathes the horizon in shades of amber and crimson, oblivious to the natural wonder mere steps away. Beneath its seemingly humorous surface, the picture delivers a sobering commentary on the digital age.

Admittedly, smartphones have revolutionized communication and information access in ways our grandparents could scarcely have imagined. Yet this technological intimacy comes at a hidden cost. When every spare moment is filled with notifications and feeds, our senses grow dull to the subtle beauty around us — the changing light of a late afternoon, the laughter of children in a distant park, or the quiet reassurance of a friend's company.

Only by deliberately lifting our eyes from the screen can we reclaim the capacity for wonder that makes life worth living. As Thoreau reminds us, "It is not what you look at that matters, it is what you see."

（约 175 词）

# 人工阅卷批注（漫画题 CET-6 14 档）

## 漫画题三要素覆盖

| 要素 | 本文表现 | 评价 |
|------|---------|------|
| ① 描述画面 | 非谓语独立主格 `his gaze fixed on... strides past... oblivious to` | ✅ 齐备 + 极其地道 |
| ② 解读含义 | `Beneath its seemingly humorous surface... sobering commentary on the digital age` | ✅ 齐备 + 有层次感（表象 → 深层）|
| ③ 给出评论 | `Only by deliberately lifting... can we reclaim...` + 梭罗名言 | ✅ 齐备 + 格言收束 |

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 14 档）

- **切题度**：切题。三段功能分工明确
- **表达清晰度**：清楚。辩证层次"Admittedly... Yet..."展开深刻
- **连贯性**：连贯。隐性衔接为主（`Admittedly / Yet / Only by`），无机械连词堆砌
- **语言准确度**：1-2 处 tip
- **CET-6 14 档硬指标全部达标**：
  - ✅ **非谓语 ≥ 3**：`his gaze fixed`（独立主格）/ `oblivious to`（形容词短语后置）/ `filled with notifications`（过去分词）
  - ✅ **倒装**：`Only by deliberately lifting our eyes from the screen can we reclaim`
  - ✅ **辩证让步**：`Admittedly, smartphones have revolutionized... Yet this technological intimacy comes at a hidden cost`
  - ✅ **高级搭配**：`poignant scene` / `sobering commentary` / `technological intimacy` / `capacity for wonder` / `subtle beauty`
  - ✅ **名言收尾 + 排比意象**（三个 the+名词并列）

## 标注的小瑕

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶1 中 | `bathes the horizon in shades of amber and crimson` | 文学感强但略过度修饰，考场作文可接受 | tip | 风格（非错误）|

## 为什么 14 分不是 15 分

`delta = 0`（给档中位 14）的理由：

- 三要素 + 辩证层 + 格言 + 非谓语/倒装/排比全部到位
- 用词达到 CET-6 样卷顶格水平（接近 `Economist`/`The Atlantic` 风格）
- 但 ¶1 "bathes the horizon in shades of amber and crimson" 风格略过度修饰，对考场作文而言"炫技"感稍强，未到"分寸完美"的 15 分水平
