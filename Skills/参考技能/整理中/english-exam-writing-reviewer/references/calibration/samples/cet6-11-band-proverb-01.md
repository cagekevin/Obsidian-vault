# 样例作文原文

```
The saying "Give a man a fish, and you feed him for a day; teach a man to fish,
and you feed him for a lifetime" tells us a simple but deep truth. It means that
giving someone food only solves a short-time problem, but teaching someone a
skill can help him for his whole life. On a deeper level, this saying suggests
that true help is not about short-term gifts, but about long-term empowerment.

A good example can be found in poverty relief. In some rural areas, the
government did not only give money to poor families. Instead, officials taught
local people how to plant mushrooms or raise chickens, so they could earn
money by themselves. A few years later, many of these families became well-off.
This shows that skills are more valuable than free food.

This saying reminds us that in education, charity and even parenting, we should
focus on building people's abilities rather than just solving their short-term
needs. Only in this way can people truly stand on their own feet.
```

（正文约 187 词）

# 人工阅卷批注（CET-6 · proverb · 11 档）

## 名言警句题型独有检查项（v1.6 新增双核判定）

| 检查项 | 合格要求 | 本文表现 | 判定 |
|-------|---------|---------|------|
| 保留名言原文 | 至少引述一次 | `The saying "Give a man a fish..." ` | ✅ 原文完整引用 |
| 字面含义解释（¶1 前半）| `means that` / `literally means` 还原字面 | `It means that giving someone food only solves a short-time problem...` | ✅ 有字面还原 |
| 深层引申（¶1 后半）| `suggests` / `implies` 提炼道理 | `On a deeper level, this saying suggests that true help is...` | ✅ 有深层引申 |
| 例证与名言相关性 | 例证能明确回扣名言主旨 | 扶贫授技术（教种蘑菇 / 养鸡）= "授人以渔" | ✅ 例证贴切 |
| 结尾回扣名言 | `This saying reminds us that` / `The wisdom lies in` | `This saying reminds us that...` | ✅ 回扣 |

→ **双核（字面解释 + 深层引申）完整触发**，这是名言警句 11 档的关键门槛。按 `cet-subtypes.md` §2 规则，若漏掉前 2-3 项之一，会直接触发 `theme_deviation` 降档。

## 隐含维度诊断

- **切题度**：切题。紧扣"授人以渔"主题，解释 + 例证 + 启示结构完整
- **表达清晰度**：思想清楚。三段论明确
- **连贯性**：文字连贯。`A good example can be found` / `This saying reminds us` 衔接自然
- **语言准确度**：3 warning + 2 tip，无 critical
- **CET-6 专有观察**：用词偏基础（`short-time / simple but deep / well-off`），未达 CET-6 14 档的"地道搭配"

## 标注错误

| 位置 | 原文 | 建议 | 分级 | 类型 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| ¶1 中 | `short-time problem` | `short-term problem` / `immediate need` | warning | lexical（`short-time` 为中式英语，正确搭配是 `short-term`）|
| ¶1 首 | `a simple but deep truth` | `a simple yet profound truth` | warning | lexical（`deep truth` 搭配生硬，`profound` 是标准搭配）|
| ¶2 末 | `became well-off` | `achieved financial self-sufficiency` | warning | lexical（`well-off` 偏口语，CET-6 11 档建议 mid-high tier）|
| ¶3 末 | `stand on their own feet` | 可保留 | tip | 成语使用到位，不扣分 |
| ¶2 中 | `so they could earn money by themselves` | `enabling them to generate income independently` | tip | syntactic（可用非谓语升档到 14）|

合计 0 critical + 3 warning + 1 tip — 符合 CET-6 11 档"少量语言错误"，但词汇层级卡在 low-mid tier。

## 为什么 11 分不是 14 分（proverb 视角 + CET-6 专属）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 档次指向 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 名言双核结构（解释+引申）| 完整 | 14 |
| 例证贴切度 | 扶贫教技术，完美对应"授人以渔" | 14 |
| 词汇层级（CET-6 14 要求 high + academic）| `short-time / simple but deep / well-off` 全在 low-mid tier | **11**（14 档要求 `short-term / profound / financially self-sufficient`）|
| 复杂句式（CET-6 14 要求非限定/倒装/强调 ≥ 2 处）| 全文复合句，但无非限定/倒装/强调 | **11**（缺复杂结构） |
| 辩证性 | 无反驳视角（如"不是所有人都能学会技能"）| 11（14 档鼓励辩证） |

→ 结构完整但词汇和句式未达 CET-6 14 档门槛，定 11 档。

## 为什么 11 分不是 8 分

- ✅ 名言双核结构完整（8 档典型表现是**跳过字面解释直接举例**，触发降档）
- ✅ 例证具体（8 档常用"many examples show" 泛泛而谈）
- ✅ 结尾回扣名言（8 档常喊口号 "Let's work hard"）
- ✅ 无 critical 错误

→ 明显优于 8 档。

## 升档路径（CET-6 proverb 11 → 14）

1. **词汇 CET-6 层级升档**：
   - `short-time problem` → `short-term predicament`
   - `a simple but deep truth` → `a simple yet profound piece of wisdom`
   - `became well-off` → `attained financial self-sufficiency`
   - `earn money by themselves` → `generate income independently`
2. **加入非限定/倒装**（CET-6 14 硬门槛）：
   - `officials taught local people how to plant mushrooms...` → `officials, rather than merely distributing funds, taught local people how to cultivate mushrooms...`
   - 结尾加倒装：`Only by empowering individuals can we foster genuine long-term prosperity.`
3. **加辩证段**（CET-6 14 鼓励）：
   - `Admittedly, not everyone possesses the aptitude to acquire new skills swiftly; nevertheless, the principle of empowerment...` 一句让步+转折即可升到 14
4. **替换机械衔接**：
   - `This shows that` → `This case exemplifies that`
   - `A good example can be found in` → `A case in point lies in`
5. **结尾升级**：
   - `in education, charity and even parenting` → `across pedagogy, philanthropy and pedagogy of parenthood`
