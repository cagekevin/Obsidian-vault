# 样例作文原文

A successful great man said: "a soldier who don't want to be a general is not a good soldier". We can find that most of men want to become great man, and do great things! But an army has only one general, most of soldiers can only be a normal soldier. So should the soldiers all go back home, just because they can't become a general? Of course not! Everyone has his value in his position. An army can't win battle if has only a general but no soldiers. If you are a soldier, just do your best, stay in your position and everyone will respect you include the general. As my father, although he's working in the government as a normal public servant not an officer, but he did his best in work. He solved lots of questions for his department, and served for hundreds of people a week to help them, he feels that he's successful because his leader and all the people he served said to him: "you are a great man".

So we just do our best in our work, do more for the others! We are all the great man!

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断

- **切题度**：**基本切题**（不是"切题"）。主题方向对但立场模糊，引用名言后未紧扣讨论
- **表达清晰度**：有些地方**不够清楚**（¶1 后半"An army can't win battle if has only a general..." 缺主语；"everyone will respect you include the general" 结构混乱）
- **连贯性**：**勉强连贯**。全文仅 2 段、缺段落划分；内部句子跳跃
- **语言准确度**：**相当多错误，有严重错误**

## 严重错误（critical，触发 8 档关键）

1. `most of men` → `most men`（冠词/名词用法错误，且重复 2 次）critical
2. `An army can't win battle if has only a general but no soldiers`（if 从句缺主语）critical
3. `everyone will respect you include the general` → `everyone, including the general, will respect you` critical（句子结构错乱）
4. `As my father`（不能用 As 表"以...为例"）critical

## 其它错误（warning 级）

- `a soldier who don't want` → `doesn't want`（主谓一致）
- `he's working...but he did his best`（时态混用：现在进行 vs 一般过去）
- `served for hundreds of people`（介词冗余）
- `he feels that he's successful because his leader...said`（时态错）
- 全文只分 2 段，第 1 段过长（discourse critical）

## 为什么 8 分不是 11 分（关键边界）

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 11 档要求 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 切题 | 基本切题（立场模糊）| 切题 |
| 严重错误 | ≥ 4 处 | ≤ 1 处 |
| 表达清晰 | 局部不够清楚 | 清楚 |
| 段落结构 | 2 段 + 首段过长 | 清晰段落 |

→ 4 项全部指向 8 档，明显不达 11 档。

## 为什么 8 分不是 5 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 5 档描述 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 基本切题 | ✓ 是 | ✓ |
| 表达思想不清楚 | "不够清楚"（非"不清楚"） | 不清楚 |
| 连贯性 | 勉强连贯 | 连贯性差 |
| 严重错误占比 | ~20% | ≥ 30% |

→ 2 项指向 8（优于 5），全文仍有可识别的论证结构。

## 升档路径（8 → 11）

1. 消除所有 critical 级语法错误（especially 句子残缺）
2. 切题要求：¶1 末给出清晰立场（"每个人都应在自己的岗位上尽力"）
3. 正确分段：引入 / 论证 / 举例 / 结论各独立段落
4. 替换中式表达（`most of men` / `great man`）
5. 统一时态：父亲的例证用一般过去时，总结用一般现在时
