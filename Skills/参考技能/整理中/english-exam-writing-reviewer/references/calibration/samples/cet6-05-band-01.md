# 样例作文原文

In the times of internet, reading is very different from past. Some people like read on phone, others still like books. Both has advantages and disadvantages.

The internet make read fast and convenient. We can find many articles on it, and it is free. But some articles are not real, may be fake news. So we should careful when read on internet. Besides, read on phone hurt our eyes. Long time use phone, we feel tired. My classmate, he always read novel on phone, now he wear glasses.

Books is more traditional, you can write note on it, and take it to anywhere. But books is expensive and heavy. So both of them has good and bad. We should choose depend on situation. If serious read, choose books. If just for fun, phone is OK.

In short, reading is important no matter in what way. It is a good habit we should keep. Don't give up reading just because you feel tired.

（约 165 词）

# 人工阅卷批注

## 隐含维度诊断（CET-6 5 档基准）

- **切题度**：**基本切题**。主题方向对（internet 与读书习惯），但立场反复摇摆——¶2 先列优点，再列缺点；¶3 再次"choose depend on situation"；¶4 又跳到"reading is important"，立场从头到尾未收拢
- **表达清晰度**：**不清楚**（已不再是"不够清楚"）。多个句子需读两遍仍不明意（"Long time use phone, we feel tired" / "choose depend on situation" / "If serious read, choose books"）
- **连贯性**：**差**。段内句子并列堆砌，无实质逻辑递进；段间无衔接
- **语言准确度**：**较多严重错误**（10+ 处 critical）

## 严重错误（critical，占比 ≥ 30%）

1. ¶1 `from past` → `from the past`（冠词缺失）
2. ¶1 `like read on phone` → `like reading on phone`（like + doing）
3. ¶1 `Both has advantages` → `Both have advantages`（主谓一致）
4. ¶2 `The internet make read fast and convenient` → `The Internet makes reading fast and convenient`（主谓一致 + 动名词）
5. ¶2 `may be fake news` → `maybe they are fake news` 或独立句（句子结构错乱）
6. ¶2 `we should careful when read` → `we should be careful when reading`（缺系动词 + 动名词）
7. ¶2 `read on phone hurt our eyes` → `reading on phone hurts our eyes`（动名词 + 主谓）
8. ¶2 `Long time use phone, we feel tired` → `After using phones for a long time, we feel tired`（句子结构完全错乱）
9. ¶3 `Books is more traditional` → `Books are more traditional`（主谓一致）
10. ¶3 `But books is expensive` → `But books are expensive`（主谓一致）
11. ¶3 `both of them has good and bad` → `both have pros and cons`（主谓 + 词性）
12. ¶3 `choose depend on situation` → `choose depending on the situation`（非谓语 + 冠词）
13. ¶3 `If serious read, choose books` → `If you read seriously, choose books`（缺主语 + 词性）

## 其它错误

- ¶2 `My classmate, he always read novel on phone` → `My classmate always reads novels on his phone`（语法冗余 + 主谓 + 单复数）
- ¶2 `now he wear glasses` → `now he wears glasses`（主谓）
- ¶3 `write note on it, and take it to anywhere` → `write notes on it, and take it anywhere`（单复数 + 介词冗余）

## 为什么 5 分不是 8 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 8 档要求 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 表达清晰度 | 不清楚（多数段落）| 不够清楚（局部）|
| 连贯性 | 差（无逻辑递进）| 勉强连贯 |
| 严重错误占比 | ≥ 35%（13+ critical / ~25 句） | < 30% |
| 论证逻辑 | 立场前后反复 | 尚可辨认 |
| 句子完整度 | 约 20% 句子结构严重残缺 | 多数句子完整 |

→ 5 项全部指向 5 档，错误密度显著超 8 档。

## 为什么 5 分不是 2 分

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 2 档描述 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 基本切题 | ✓（勉强，主题方向始终围绕 internet/reading）| 条理不清 + 可能离题 |
| 论证结构 | 有可识别的三段（优点 / 缺点 / 总结）| 思路紊乱 |
| 句子完整度 | 多数句子至少主谓齐全（虽然错误百出）| 支离破碎 |
| 拼写 | 除结构问题外无严重拼写错 | 拼写错误高发 |

→ 4 项指向 5 档，尚未至"条理不清、支离破碎"。

## 升档路径（CET-6 5 → 8）

1. **先保"基本切题 + 立场一致"**：
   - 开篇直接表态（"我认为网络阅读是必要的补充但不能取代传统阅读"），全文围绕此立场
2. **消灭 5-7 处最严重的结构性错误**（优先级：主谓一致、like/enjoy + doing、缺系动词）
3. **统一时态**（全文用一般现在时讨论观点）
4. **分段清晰**（当前 4 段但段内跳跃）→ 每段一个中心论点
5. **替换中式直译**：
   - `from past` → `from the past`
   - `Long time use phone` → `Using phones for long periods`
   - `choose depend on situation` → `make the choice according to the situation`
6. **CET-6 特定要求**：本档距 CET-6 11 档差距巨大，建议先冲 8 档
