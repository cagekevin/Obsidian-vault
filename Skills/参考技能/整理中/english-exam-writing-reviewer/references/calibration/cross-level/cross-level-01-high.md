# 跨级别对照 01 · 高水平作文（Bill Gates saying）

## 作文原文（复用 `cet4-14-band-01.md`）

> We all remember a time when we spoke proudly about becoming the next Bill Gates, or Stephen Hawking, and it's never wrong to dream big. As time passes by, we gradually realize that most of us are bound to stay ordinary. Yet there's still a way of achieving excellency. As the saying goes, "If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way."
>
> A perfect life doesn't have to be so glorious or sparkling as grand plays performed in theatres. The true value lies in our attitude towards tiny daily issues, of which the repairman working in our community is an ideal example. Bicycle repairing is certainly not the brightest or best-paid profession to many, but this man puts his heart and soul into it and views it as his way of serving for people in the community, which is why he earns high reputation.
>
> As a generation about to step into the fiercely competitive society, we are haunted by the feeling of worry and helplessness. If we strive for accomplishing remarkable success and becoming world-renowned, we'll probably feel frustrated and disappointed. Only by realizing "Excellency derives from small matters" can we survive and thrive.

**字数**：约 175 词

---

## 一、作为 CET-4 提交 → **14 分**（已有判定，见 `cet4-14-band-01.md`）

- 用词地道：`strive for` / `put heart and soul into` / `haunted by` / `world-renowned`
- 句式多样：非谓语 `performed` + 定从 `of which` + 倒装 `Only by... can we...`
- 2 处 tip（`excellency` 拼写 + `serving for people` 介词冗余）
- 判定：**14 分**（CET-4 14 档中位）

## 二、作为 CET-6 提交 → **13 分**（14 档档内下位）

### 2.1 按 CET-6 14 档硬指标逐项核查

| CET-6 14 档硬指标 | 本文是否达成 | 证据 |
|-------------------|:----------:|------|
| 非谓语结构 ≥ 3 处 | ✅ | `performed in theatres`、`working in our community`、`about to step into`、`accomplishing remarkable success...` — 共 4+ 处 |
| 倒装 ≥ 1 处 | ✅ | `Only by realizing... can we survive and thrive` |
| 辩证 / 让步 | ⚠️ 不足 | 仅有 `Yet there's still a way` 作弱让步，无 `Admittedly... Yet...` 正式辩证结构 |
| 高级搭配密度 | ✅ | `strive for` / `put heart and soul into` / `haunted by` / `world-renowned` / `derives from` |
| 格言 / 名言收尾 | ✅ | "Excellency derives from small matters" |
| 错误密度 ≤ 2 处小错 | ✅ | 2 处 tip |

→ **6 项中 5 项达标，1 项偏弱**（辩证让步略弱）。

### 2.2 CET-6 下的额外失分点

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | CET-6 14 档基准 | 影响 |
|-------|---------|---------------|------|
| 论证层次 | 观点 → 例证（repairman）→ 升华 | CET-6 14 档应有"观点 → 反驳 → 让步 → 升华"的四层结构 | -0.5 |
| 辩证让步强度 | `Yet there's still...` 弱让步 | CET-6 14 档期待 `Admittedly, it is human nature to aspire to greatness, yet...` | -0.5 |
| 例证深度 | 一个 repairman 例证 | CET-6 14 档期待 2 个层次不同的例证（普通职业 + 知识分子型职业）| -0.3 |
| 用词 `excellency` | 拼写不规范 | CET-6 级别对拼写更敏感 | 小扣分 |

### 2.3 最终判定

```
CET-6 判定：14 档档内下位
CET-6 原始分：13 分（档内 ±1 调整：delta = -1）
CET-6 报告分：13 × 7.1 = 92.3 分
```

### 2.4 rationale

> 本文在 CET-6 样卷基准下仍属于 14 档（语言准确度、句式复杂度、词汇地道度都达标），
> 但辩证层次与例证深度未达到 CET-6 14 档顶格要求。因此定 14 档下位（13 分），
> 而不是 CET-4 视角下的中位 14 分。
