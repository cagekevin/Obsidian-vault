# 跨级别对照 02 · 中等作文（Miss Chen）

## 作文原文（复用 `cet4-11-band-01.md`）

> "If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way" is the greatest saying I've ever heard. I admire many people. Some of them are heroes who do great things, but some of them are ordinary people. They do small things which seems simple and boring.
>
> To illustrate it, I show an example first. My Chinese teacher, Miss Chen, is an ordinary senior high school teacher. She teaches everyday. However, in my eyes, she's a great teacher. She really loves her students and her courses are wonderful. She will spend her spare time to prepare a good class. She's always willing to help students. As a result, she's a very popular teacher in our school.
>
> Miss Chen doesn't do great things, but she tries to make her job great. When you do things wholeheartedly, you are great person. Obviously, not everyone has the chance to be a hero, but when we do our small things in great way, we succeed.
>
> This is an amazing quality. So we shouldn't complain that we can't do great things. Let's do small things in a great way to be our own hero.

**字数**：约 180 词

## 一、作为 CET-4 提交 → **11 分**（已有判定）

- 切题 + 立场一致 + 具体例证（Miss Chen）
- 3 warning + 1 tip（主谓一致、时态、冠词、语气偏口语）
- 衔接略机械（`obviously / as a result / so`）
- 用词常用（`greatest saying / amazing quality`）
- 判定：**11 分**（CET-4 11 档中位）

## 二、作为 CET-6 提交 → **8 分**（降 1 档）

### 2.1 按 CET-6 11 档硬指标逐项核查（是否能稳住 11 档）

| CET-6 11 档要求 | 本文是否达成 | 证据 |
|---------------|:----------:|------|
| 切题 + 立场一致 | ✅ | Miss Chen 贯穿全文 |
| 清晰结构 | ✅ | 4 段式分明 |
| 词汇丰富度 | ❌ | 常用词堆砌 `great / amazing / wonderful`，无高级搭配 |
| 句式适度变化 | ❌ | 几乎全部简单句，仅一个定从 `which seems simple and boring` |
| 错误 ≤ 2-4 处小错 | ⚠️ 踩线 | 3 warning + 1 tip（CET-4 11 档容忍 3-6 处，CET-6 11 档只容忍 2-4 处）|
| 论证深度 | ❌ | 例证有但停留"她爱学生"的表面层 |

→ **7 项中仅 2 项达标**，5 项不达标。

### 2.2 CET-6 下的关键失分点

| 判定点 | 本文表现 | 档次指向 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 词汇丰富度 | `great / amazing / wonderful / popular` 常用词循环使用 | CET-6 8 档（未展现 6000 词汇库）|
| 句式 | 近乎全部简单+复合句 | CET-6 8 档（11 档期待复杂句）|
| 论证深度 | 例证单薄（"she loves her students"）| CET-6 8 档（11 档期待"为什么这叫做小事做好"的深度分析）|
| 语气 | `Let's do small things... to be our own hero` 口语化祈使句 | CET-6 8 档（11 档期待书面语）|
| 错误密度 | 3 warning + 1 tip（刚好卡 CET-6 11 档上限）| 介于 8-11 之间 |

### 2.3 为什么是 8 档而不是 11 档

**核心判定**：CET-6 11 档的关键门槛是"词汇 + 句式达到 CET-6 水平"，本文在这两项上明显不达标。

但也不至于 5 档：
- ✅ 切题（非"基本切题"）
- ✅ 无 critical 结构错误
- ✅ 段落结构清晰
- ✅ 论证有清晰主线

→ **CET-6 下判 8 档**：基本切题（切题但论证偏浅）、表达基本清楚、有少量错误但词汇句式达不到 CET-6 11 档基准。

```
CET-6 判定：8 档中位
CET-6 原始分：8 分（delta = 0）
CET-6 报告分：8 × 7.1 = 56.8 分
```

### 2.5 rationale

> 本文作为 CET-4 提交是稳健的 11 分作文（切题、结构清晰、例证具体、少量小错）。
> 但作为 CET-6 提交时，"词汇全部为常用词 + 几乎无复杂句式 + 例证停留表面"这三

## 四、CET-6 视角下的升档路径（8 → 11）

如果这名考生想在 CET-6 拿回 11 档：

1. **用词升级**（最优先）：
   - `great` → `remarkable` / `admirable`
   - `amazing` → `inspiring` / `commendable`
   - `wonderful` → `exceptional` / `engaging`
   - `popular` → `well-respected`
2. **加入复杂句式**（至少 2 处）：
   - `She teaches everyday. However, in my eyes, she's a great teacher.` → `Although her daily teaching may appear unremarkable to outsiders, in my eyes Miss Chen exemplifies true greatness.`
   - `When you do things wholeheartedly, you are great person.` → `By devoting oneself wholeheartedly to any pursuit, one attains a form of greatness that transcends scale.`
3. **深化论证**：
   - 替换 `She really loves her students` 为"她如何具体体现爱"——比如"She remembers every student's name and their weak points within the first month."
4. **替换机械衔接**：
   - `Obviously → It is evident that`
   - `As a result → Consequently`
   - `So → Thus / In this regard`
5. **书面化语气**：
   - `Let's do small things...` → `We ought to pursue excellence in small things...`
