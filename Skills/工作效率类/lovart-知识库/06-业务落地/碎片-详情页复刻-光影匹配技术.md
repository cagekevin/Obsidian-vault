## 一、光影要素的Prompt描述方法
### 1. **光源方向描述**
```
• 正面光：front lighting, flat lighting, direct frontal light
• 侧光：side lighting, 45-degree side light, cross lighting
• 顶光：top lighting, overhead light, high-angle lighting
• 逆光：backlight, rim light, contour lighting
• 底光：bottom lighting, under lighting
• 多方向：three-point lighting, multi-directional lighting
```
### 2. **光质（软硬光）描述**
```
硬光（Hard Light）：
• hard light, sharp shadows, direct sunlight
• crisp edge shadows, high contrast lighting
• spotlight effect, focused beam
软光（Soft Light）：
• soft diffused light, gentle lighting
• softbox lighting, diffused shadows
• overcast light, studio softbox
• gradient shadows, subtle lighting transition
```
### 3. **色温描述**
```
暖色调：
• warm lighting, golden hour light (2700-3500K)
• warm white light, sunset glow
• amber lighting, cozy warm tone
中性色：
• neutral white light, daylight balanced (5000-5500K)
• natural daylight, studio white light
冷色调：
• cool white light, blue-toned lighting (6000-7000K)
• cool daylight, crisp white light
• clinical white light
```
### 4. **阴影描述**
```
阴影特征：
• soft shadow with gradual falloff（柔和渐变阴影）
• sharp defined shadow（锐利清晰阴影）
• minimal shadow, barely visible shadow（极少阴影）
• long cast shadow（长投影）
• contact shadow only（仅接触阴影）
• shadow density 30%（阴影密度30%）
```

## 三、复杂光影的分步处理技巧
### **技巧1：分层描述法**
当原图光影复杂时，按层级拆解：
```
Step 1 - 主光源：
"main key light from front-right 30-degree angle, hard light, creating primary shadow on left side"
Step 2 - 补光：
"soft fill light from left at 40% intensity, reducing shadow harshness"
Step 3 - 环境光：
"ambient reflected light from white surface below, subtle bottom fill"
Step 4 - 特殊光效：
"small accent light on product logo area, spotlight effect"
完整组合Prompt：
"main key light from front-right 30-degree creating primary shadows, soft fill light from left at 40% intensity, ambient reflected light from below, accent spotlight on logo, professional product photography lighting"
```
### **技巧2：参考图+文字增强法**
```
工作流程：
1. 使用原图作为reference image
2. Prompt中强调："matching the exact lighting setup from reference image"
3. 补充具体光影描述：
   "replicate the soft diffused lighting with shadows falling to bottom-right, 
   maintain the same highlight position on top-left surface, 
   preserve the gentle shadow gradient, 
   match the warm color temperature around 3200K"
```
### **技巧3：阴影精确控制**
```
阴影方向：
"shadow casting towards bottom-right at 45-degree angle"
阴影距离：
"shadow extending 15% of product width"
阴影清晰度：
"shadow with 60% edge softness, gradual falloff"
阴影透明度：
"shadow opacity at 35%, semi-transparent"
完整示例：
"soft shadow casting bottom-right, extending 15% of product width, 60% edge blur, 35% opacity, natural shadow falloff"
```
### **技巧4：材质与光影联动**
不同材质需要匹配的光影描述：
```
金属材质：
"hard directional light creating sharp specular highlights, 
crisp reflection, high contrast between lit and shadow areas"
磨砂材质：
"soft diffused light, subtle highlight with no sharp reflection, 
gentle shadow transition, matte surface lighting"
透明材质：
"backlight to show transparency, light passing through material, 
internal light refraction, glowing edges"
布料材质：
"soft wrap-around lighting, gentle shadows showing fabric texture, 
diffused light revealing weave pattern"
```
### **技巧5：多角度迭代法**
```
第一次生成：
"基础光影描述 + studio lighting"
观察结果后第二次优化：
"基础描述 + 增加具体阴影角度 + 调整光源强度比例"
第三次精修：
"完整描述 + 参考图 + 强调关键光影特征（如'emphasize the soft shadow 
gradient on the right side, maintain bright highlight on top surface'）"
```

## 四、实战检查清单
复刻光影时，逐项对比原图检查：
- [ ] **光源数量**：单光源/双光源/多光源
- [ ] **主光方向**：前/侧/顶/逆（具体角度）
- [ ] **光质软硬**：硬光/软光/混合
- [ ] **色温**：暖/中性/冷（K值范围）
- [ ] **阴影位置**：方向、距离、形状
- [ ] **阴影清晰度**：锐利/模糊程度
- [ ] **阴影浓度**：深/中/浅（透明度%）
- [ ] **高光位置**：在产品哪个面
- [ ] **环境反射**：有无底部/侧面反射光
