# 品牌全局样式红线

## 字体组 (Font Pairings)
- elegant (default): Title(Didot Bold), Subtitle(Didot Regular), Body(Montserrat)
- modern: Title(Cormorant Garamond), Subtitle(Cormorant Regular), Body(Lato)
- bold: Title(Playfair Display), Subtitle(Playfair Regular), Body(Raleway)

## 文本层颜色规范 (Text Colors)
- Title/Subtitle/Body: dark red-brown (#4A1A1A)
- Data numbers / arrows / Section labels: gold (#C8A45C)
- Disclaimer: grey (#999999)
- Brand logo: HKH, gold, serif (#C8A45C)
