# 提示词实战经验

> 基于诗意写实（Lyrical Realism）风格的真实案例：岩玫瑰海岸场景 6 次迭代过程。
> 下次出图前先读这个，直接避开已知坑。

---

## 核心教训：踩过的坑

| 迭代 | 做了什么 | 结果 | 诊断 |
|------|---------|------|------|
| 第1次 | 详细技术描述 + vivid colors + professional composition | ❌ "太丑了" | 像旅游明信片，刻意摆拍感 |
| 第2次 | 加入 Wim Wenders + melancholic + film grain | ❌ "像假花" | 花朵质感失真，塑料感 |
| 第3次 | 植物学精确描述：filaments, anthers, pollen | ❌ "越来越差了" | 技术术语让 AI 混乱 |
| 第4次 | 换工具 nano_banana_2 + 结构化诗意写实 | ✅ "比较真实了，但缺诗意" | 写实达成，诗意不足 |
| 第5次 | 注入情绪化诗意：filtered through gauze, trembling, breathes | ✅ "还不错，海面太平" | 诗意达成，动态不足 |
| 第6次 | 微调海面动态：low swells, ripples and foam, waves folding | ✅ "基本达到要求" | 真实+诗意+动态平衡 |

---

## 三个关键突破

### 1. 工具比提示词更重要
- `gpt_image_2` → 无论怎么改都"太丑"
- 换成 `nano_banana_2` → 同样描述立刻"比较真实了"
- **教训**：提示词反复优化仍失败时，问题可能在工具，不在词

### 2. 技术术语是毒药
- ❌ `individual filaments visible, plump anthers, pollen scattered`
- ✅ `golden stamens catching the pale light`
- **教训**：AI 需要"视觉画面"，不是"植物学教科书"

### 3. 诗意 = 动词 + 比喻 + 情绪
- 第4版缺什么：只有准确描述，没有感受
- 第5版加了什么：
  - **动词**：trembling, breathing, dissolving
  - **比喻**：filtered through gauze, paper-thin like silk
  - **情绪**：A moment suspended, unguarded and tender

## 最终有效的提示词结构

```
[时间+地点+光线情绪]
[前景主体：形态+质感比喻+细节]
[自然姿态：反摆拍]
[中景：动态+质感]
[远景：空间消融]
[空气触感]
[色调+情绪+时间感]
[技术标签]
```

### 完整成功案例

```
A quiet morning by the coast, light soft and diffused as if filtered through gauze.
In the foreground, wild Cistus ladanifer blooms rest among salt-worn grasses—
five white petals, paper-thin and crinkled like silk,
each marked with a dark wine-red blotch at its heart,
golden stamens catching the pale light.
The flowers lean naturally, some facing away, petals trembling in the cool sea breeze.
Beyond them, a grey-blue sea rolls gently with low swells,
surface textured with soft ripples and foam, waves folding quietly against dark rocks.
Distant shoreline dissolving into silver mist.
The air feels damp and still.
Muted palette of whites, greys, and faded greens.
A moment suspended, unguarded and tender.
Shot on film, subtle grain, 3:4.
```

## 词汇对比表（实测有效 vs 无效）

### 光线

| ❌ 无效 | ✅ 有效 |
|---------|---------|
| bright sunlight | light soft and diffused |
| professional lighting | as if filtered through gauze |
| dramatic light | catching the pale light |

### 质感

| ❌ 无效 | ✅ 有效 |
|---------|---------|
| individual filaments visible | golden stamens |
| plump anthers | paper-thin and crinkled like silk |
| pollen scattered on petals | dusting of pollen |

### 动态

| ❌ 无效 | ✅ 有效 |
|---------|---------|
| flowers swaying | petals trembling |
| ocean moving | sea rolls gently, waves folding quietly |
| water flowing | surface textured with soft ripples and foam |

### 构图

| ❌ 无效 | ✅ 有效 |
|---------|---------|
| professional three-layer composition | （不提"专业构图"，直接描述层次） |
| perfectly arranged | lean naturally, some facing away |
| facing camera | unguarded, as if glimpsed in passing |

### 色彩

| ❌ 无效 | ✅ 有效 |
|---------|---------|
| vivid colors | muted palette |
| bright and colorful | whites, greys, and faded greens |
| saturated | grey-blue, silver mist |

## 实战修正流程

### 问题：太丑 / 像明信片
- **删除**：vivid, bright, professional, perfect
- **添加**：muted, faded, naturally, some facing away

### 问题：像假花 / 不真实
- **删除**：技术术语（filaments, anthers）
- **添加**：比喻（paper-thin like silk, crinkled）
- **关键**：换工具到 nano_banana_2

### 问题：缺少诗意感
- **添加**：诗意动词（trembling, breathing, dissolving）
- **添加**：光的比喻（filtered through gauze）
- **添加**：情绪收尾（A moment suspended, unguarded and tender）

### 问题：太平 / 太静
- **添加**：具体动态描述（low swells, soft ripples and foam, waves folding quietly）
- **注意**：不要用 dramatic，要用 quietly

## 🔍 强制审计：提示词前后一致性检查

**写完提示词后，AI 必须逐项审计，确保无前后矛盾。**

从词汇对比表中提取规则，检查提示词中是否混用了 ❌ 列和 ✅ 列的词汇。

### 审计维度

| # | 检查维度 | 说明 |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | 色调 | 不能混用 ❌ vivid/bright 和 ✅ muted/faded |
| 2 | 光感 | 不能混用 ❌ dramatic 和 ✅ soft/diffused |
| 3 | 姿态 | 不能混用 ❌ professional/perfect 和 ✅ naturally/unguarded |
| 4 | 视角 | 不能混用 ❌ looking at viewer 和 ✅ candid/unguarded |
| 5 | 质感 | 不能混用 ❌ sharp/ultra-detailed 和 ✅ film grain/soft |
| 6 | 情绪 | 不能混用 ❌ epic/dramatic 和 ✅ tender/melancholic |

> 具体冲突案例留空，等实际遇到再补。

### 审计流程

```
1. 提取提示词中的风格关键词
2. 对照上表检查是否有冲突对
3. 发现冲突 → 删除无效列的词，保留有效列的词
4. 确认全部通过 → 输出最终提示词
```

## 为文字预留区域的设计技巧

> 来源：2026-06-23 小红书岩玫瑰溯源项目实战。目标是在画面中自然地形成色调均匀、适合添加文字的区域，不能有刻意留白的痕迹。

### 推荐做法

**1. 用天气/光线现象**
```
heat haze creating soft, even tones
uniform overcast sky
soft diffused light creating even background
```

**2. 用本身就均匀的元素**
```
plain white wall
smooth concrete surface
calm water surface
clean wooden table
uniform sand
solid color backdrop
```

核心：这些元素本身就是均匀的，自然适合写字。

**3. 用景深虚化**
```
background gently softened with natural depth of field
out-of-focus background creating even tones
```

**4. 用构图分区引导**
```
[主体] occupying lower portion
upper portion shows [均匀元素] with even tones
```

### 要避免的

```
❌ providing clean space
❌ blank area for text
❌ leaving room for typography
```

### 实战案例：岩玫瑰自燃场景

```
A quiet late-summer afternoon by the dry coastal scrub,
the air thick with heat and the scent of scorched earth,
heat haze creating soft, even tones across the upper frame.

In the foreground, a cluster of wild Cistus ladanifer,
some petals still white and paper-thin, others singed and curling at the edges,
dark wine-red blotches deepening to near black in the heat.
Dried stems and seed pods scattered among grey ashes,
a faint wisp of smoke rising from the embers.

Upper portion shows hazy coastline with uniform overcast sky,
the sea barely visible through the heat haze.

Muted palette of charred greys, pale whites, and faded earth tones.
A moment of stillness after fire, quiet and unguarded.
Shot on film, subtle grain, 3:4.
```

## 🔥 燃烧场景提示词铁律

> 来源：2026-06-23 岩玫瑰自燃场景踩坑记录。当提示词涉及"火/燃烧/烧焦"时，AI 容易失控，必须严格遵守以下检查清单。

### 检查清单 1：描述对象

**问题：用户说"花朵着火"，我描述的是什么？**

```
❌ "stems"、"branches"、"ashes" → 错了
✅ "flowers"、"petals"、"petal edges" → 对了
```

**规则**：先确定谁在燃烧，只描述主体的燃烧状态。不要跳到周边元素。

### 检查清单 2：动态词

**问题：提示词里有没有这些词？**

```
❌ spreading、catching fire、consuming、engulfing → 删掉
✅ with flames、occasional、a few → 保留
```

**规则**：用静态描述（with/occasional），不用动作描述（spreading/catching）。动态词会让 AI 把火画成灾难现场。

### 检查清单 3：描述比重

**问题：主体和次要元素的描述比例是多少？**

```
❌ 主体和次要描述一样多 → 失衡
✅ 主体 60%，次要 30%，环境 10% → 平衡
```

**规则**：燃烧场景下更要控制比重。主体（花朵）永远占大头，次要（灰烬/烟雾）次之，环境最后。

### 检查清单 4：对比状态

**问题：有没有描述"完整"与"破坏"的对比？**

```
❌ 只描述"烧焦" → 缺少对比
✅ some intact + others char → 有对比
```

**规则**：不要全部烧焦。用 `some...others...` 结构制造对比，保留一部分完整状态，画面才有叙事张力。

### 核心原则总结

```
🔥 燃烧场景提示词铁律
─────────────────────
主体优先    → 先确定谁在燃烧（花朵 > 树枝）
静态描述    → 用 with 不用 spreading
零星控制    → 用 a few、occasional
对比状态    → some intact vs others char
比重控制    → 主体 60%，次要 30%
```

> 词语要适当，要有结构，要诗意写实。
