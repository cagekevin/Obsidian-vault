> 在画面中自然地形成色调均匀、适合添加文字的区域，不能有刻意留白的痕迹。

---

## 推荐做法

**1. 用天气/光线现象**
```
heat haze creating soft, even tones
uniform overcast sky
soft diffused light creating even background
```

**2. 用本身就均匀的元素**
```
plain white wall
smooth concrete surface
calm water surface
clean wooden table
uniform sand
solid color backdrop
```
核心：这些元素本身就是均匀的，自然适合写字。

**3. 用景深虚化**
```
background gently softened with natural depth of field
out-of-focus background creating even tones
```

**4. 用构图分区引导**
```
[主体] occupying lower portion
upper portion shows [均匀元素] with even tones
```

---

## 要避免的

```
❌ providing clean space
❌ blank area for text
❌ leaving room for typography
```
